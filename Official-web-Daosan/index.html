company profile

Since its establishment in 2016, our company has been focusing on fasteners, metal processing parts, stamping parts, forging and casting parts, etc. The main service areas are electric power, solar photovoltaic, wind power, construction, hydraulic transmission, auto parts, etc. We export high quality fastener products such as screws, nuts, bolts, washers, etc. from China to many countries such as USA, South Korea, Japan, Germany, Greece, South Africa and so on. We meet the needs of many industries such as global manufacturing, construction, wind power generation industry and so on. Our company has a strict quality control system to ensure that our products meet international standards and customer specific requirements. The wide range of products and specifications are available to meet the needs of different industries. In addition, the company has a strong R&D capability and is able to customize non-standard products according to customers' special needs.

Hex Flanged Nuts
